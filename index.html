Region: East Honolulu, Hawaii Kai, and Kalaniana'ole Highway

Current Status: Widespread Outages (Active)

Due to severe Kona storm conditions, Hawaiian Electric is currently dealing with widespread outages affecting over 111,000 customers statewide.

  • Recent Impacts: Power was lost for over 13,000 customers from Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai and Waimanalo. Hazardous weather damaged the main transmission lines serving these areas.
  • Affected Neighborhoods: Hawaii Kai, Aina Haina, Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.
  • Estimated Restoration: HECO has warned customers to prepare for extended outages that may last overnight or for days depending on weather and damage severity. Crews are working as conditions allow.
