HECO Power Outage Status

Last Updated: March 14, 2026 - 5:39 PM UTC

⚠️ Widespread Outages in East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Hazardous weather conditions from a Kona low storm have caused significant damage to the transmission lines serving East O'ahu.

  • Affected Areas: Aina Haina, Hawaii Kai, Waimanalo, Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, Kalama Valley, and along Kalaniana'ole Highway.
  • Current Status: Power was lost again at about 9:11 p.m. last night for over 13,000 customers in the area.
  • Outlook: HECO urges customers to prepare for extended outages that may last overnight or longer, as crews can only safely restore power when conditions allow.

Please stay safe and limit travel if signals are out along Kalaniana'ole Hwy.
