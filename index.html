HECO Outage Tracker: East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

⚠️ Widespread Outage Warning
Hawaiian Electric (HECO) urges customers in Hawaii Kai and portions of East Honolulu to prepare for extended outages that may last overnight or longer due to a severe Kona low storm.

Latest Status

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

Hazardous weather conditions damaged the single remaining transmission line serving both Windward Oʻahu and the Hawaiʻi Kai and East Honolulu areas. Power went out again at about 9:11 p.m. for over 13,000 customers in the region.

Affected Areas Include:

  • Hawaii Kai
  • Aina Haina
  • Kalaniana'ole Highway corridor
  • Waimanalo
  • Hahaione
  • Hawaii Loa Ridge
  • Portlock
  • Kalama Valley

Current Guidance:

Prepare for prolonged outages that may last overnight or longer. Check home circuit breakers to ensure service issues aren't localized. Stay safe and avoid downed power lines.
