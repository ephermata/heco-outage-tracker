🚨 HECO Power Outage Status

Last Updated: March 15, 2026, 7:41 PM UTC

Current Status: Active Outages in East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Severe Kona low storms have caused significant damage to transmission lines serving the area.

Impacted Areas:

  • Hawaii Kai & East Honolulu: Over 13,000 customers experienced a renewed outage around 9:11 p.m. last night.
  • Specific Neighborhoods: Aina Haina, Hawaii Kai, Waimanalo, Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.
  • Kalaniana'ole Highway Corridor: Traffic signals and surrounding neighborhoods are likely impacted.

HECO Advisory:

Hawaiian Electric (HECO) is urging customers in Hawaii Kai and portions of East Honolulu to prepare for the possibility of extended outages that may last overnight or longer as crews work to restore the damaged transmission lines amidst ongoing severe weather.

Data sourced from local news and Hawaiian Electric updates.
