HECO Power Outage Status

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Current Situation: Recovery Mode

Following a severe Kona Low storm over the weekend, Hawaiian Electric (HECO) is actively restoring power. At the peak, over 121,000 customers were affected on Oahu.

Affected Areas: East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

  • Kalaniana'ole Highway Corridor: Major transmission lines near Waimanalo running into East Honolulu were damaged by high winds and downpours.
  • Neighborhoods Impacted: Aina Haina, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Hahaione, Portlock, and Kalama Valley experienced significant, repeated outages over the weekend.
  • Restoration Progress: As of late Sunday night, crews have restored power to the vast majority of the island, with remaining Oahu outages reduced to approximately 7,000 customers. Crews continue to repair downed poles and damaged equipment.

Next Steps: HECO advises residents still without power to check their home circuit breakers before calling the trouble line, as individual service drops may require targeted repairs.
