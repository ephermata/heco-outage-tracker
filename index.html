Hawaiian Electric (HECO) Outage Status

Last Updated: March 14, 2026, 7:09 PM (UTC)

Current Status: Severe Outages Ongoing

East Honolulu, Hawaii Kai & Kalaniana'ole Hwy Updates

  • Widespread Impact: A severe Kona low storm has caused scattered outages across Oahu affecting over 111,000 HECO customers.
  • Transmission Line Damage: Hazardous weather damaged the single remaining transmission line serving the Windward Oahu, Hawaii Kai, and East Honolulu areas.
  • Recent Outages: At around 9:11 PM on March 13, power went out again for over 13,000 customers stretching from Aina Haina and Hawaii Kai to Waimanalo, including areas like Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.
  • Extended Duration: HECO is urging customers in Hawaii Kai and portions of East Honolulu to prepare for extended outages that may last overnight or longer until crews can safely complete repairs.

Please check your home circuit breakers or fuses before reporting an outage, and stay away from downed power lines. Hawaiian Electric's trouble line is available 24/7.
