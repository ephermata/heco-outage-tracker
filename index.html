HECO Outage Status: East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Last Updated: March 15, 2026
⚠️ CRITICAL UPDATE: Extended Outages Expected

Current Situation: East Honolulu and Hawaii Kai are currently experiencing significant power outages due to severe weather from a Kona low storm. Hazardous conditions damaged a major transmission line serving these areas.

Affected Areas: Over 13,000 customers from Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai to Waimanalo (including Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley) are currently without power.

HECO Advisory: Hawaiian Electric (HECO) is urging customers in Hawaii Kai and portions of East Honolulu to prepare for the possibility of extended outages that may last overnight or longer.

Crews are working as conditions allow to safely restore the transmission lines.
