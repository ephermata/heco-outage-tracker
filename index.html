Following the recent Kona Low storm that initially knocked out power for over 13,000 customers from Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai (including Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley), HECO crews have successfully restored major transmission lines along the Kalaniana'ole Highway corridor.

What to do if you are still without power:
