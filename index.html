HECO Power Outage Update

Last Updated: March 14, 2026, 6:39 PM (UTC)
Current Status: Active Outages

Scattered outages continue to affect East Honolulu and Hawaii Kai due to the Kona low storm system.

Impacted Areas & Details

  • Hawaii Kai & Aina Haina: After initial restorations, power went out again around 9:11 p.m. yesterday for over 13,000 customers spanning Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai and Waimanalo.
  • Neighborhoods Affected: Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.
  • Kalanianaole Highway: Traffic lights may be out or intermittent. Drivers are advised to treat all impacted intersections as four-way stops.
  • Windward Oahu: Outages affecting roughly 740 customers between Waimanalo and Hawaii Kai, and 1,900 customers in Kaneohe.

Advisory

HECO crews are working around the clock to restore power as weather conditions allow. Residents should prepare for the possibility of continued or intermittent outages as strong winds and rain persist.
