HECO Power Outage Update

Last Updated: March 15, 2026, 7:41 AM (UTC)

Current Status: Major Outages Ongoing

Affected Areas: East Honolulu, Hawai'i Kai, Kalaniana'ole Highway, Windward O'ahu, and broader regions across the state.

Cause: Severe Kona storm weather conditions with strengthening winds.

Latest Details

  • More than 110,000-130,000 HECO customers statewide are currently experiencing power outages.
  • Hazardous weather damaged the transmission lines serving Windward O'ahu, Hawai'i Kai, and East Honolulu.
  • HECO is operating an "all hands on deck" response to restore power as soon as weather conditions safely permit.
  • Crews have successfully restored power to some customers (roughly 29,000 initially reported in parts of the affected area), but widespread issues remain.

Please stay safe, keep refrigerators/freezers closed, and avoid any downed power lines.
