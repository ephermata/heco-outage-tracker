⚠️ HECO Power Outage Update

Status: Active Outages in East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Areas Affected: Aina Haina, Hawaii Kai, Waimanalo, Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley (including Kalaniana'ole Highway corridor).

Latest Information:

  • Severe weather (Kona low storm) has caused significant damage to transmission lines.
  • Over 13,000 customers in the East Honolulu/Hawaii Kai area have experienced recurring outages as crews work to restore the single remaining transmission line.
  • Hawaiian Electric (HECO) has an "all-hands on deck" approach today but urges residents to remain prepared for extended or rolling blackouts.
Last Updated: March 16, 2026 07:41 UTC
