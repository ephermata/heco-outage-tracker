HECO Power Outage Update

Last Updated: March 16, 2026 - 11:41 AM (UTC)
Current Status: Active Outages & Ongoing Restoration

East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai Focus

Hawaiian Electric (HECO) has been working to restore power following severe winds. Recent reports indicate power was lost for over 13,000 customers spanning from Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai to Waimanalo, including areas such as Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.

Statewide Progress: As of the latest morning updates, crews have made significant progress. The number of customers without power on Oahu has been reduced to approximately 7,000 (about 3% of the island's customers). Major transmission lines are being repaired and major routes like the H-3 Freeway have reopened.

What to do:

  • Treat all downed power lines as energized and dangerous. Stay at least 30 feet away.
  • Monitor the official HECO Oahu Outage Map for real-time estimates.
  • Keep devices charged when power is available.
