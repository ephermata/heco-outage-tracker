Current Status: Active Outages in East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Recent severe weather (Kona low storm) has caused significant and recurring power outages impacting tens of thousands of customers statewide, including major impacts in the East Honolulu area.

Latest Updates for Kalaniana'ole Hwy & Surrounding Areas:

  • Recent Outages: Power went out again for over 13,000 customers from 'Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai and Waimanalo. Other impacted areas include Haha'ione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.
