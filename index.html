HECO Outage Updates: East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

⚠️ Active Outages Reported

Affected Areas: Aina Haina, Hawaii Kai, Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, Kalama Valley, Waimanalo (Kalaniana'ole Highway corridor).

Current Situation: Hawaiian Electric (HECO) reports ongoing outages due to a severe Kona low storm impacting transmission lines. Around 13,000 customers lost power again at approximately 9:11 p.m.

Guidance: HECO is urging customers in Hawaii Kai and portions of East Honolulu to prepare for the possibility of extended outages that may last overnight or longer.

For official real-time maps, visit the HECO Oahu Outage Map.
