Safety Reminders:

  • Treat all downed power lines as live and dangerous. Stay at least 30 feet away.
  • Keep refrigerators and freezers closed to preserve food.
  • Use flashlights instead of candles to prevent fire hazards.
  • If you have a portable generator, ensure it is operated outside in a well-ventilated area.
