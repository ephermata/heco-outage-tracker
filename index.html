HECO Power Outage Status: East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Last Updated: March 16, 2026
Current Situation: Ongoing severe weather has caused widespread outages. Hawaiian Electric crews are working around the clock as conditions allow.

Latest Updates:

  • East Honolulu / Hawaii Kai: Power went out for over 13,000 customers from Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai and Waimanalo. Affected areas include Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.
  • Statewide: Tens of thousands of customers have been impacted across the islands due to the recent storms. Restoration efforts are ongoing but may be delayed overnight in some areas due to unsafe conditions.
