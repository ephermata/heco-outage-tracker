RECENT MAJOR OUTAGES REPORTED
Last Updated: March 17, 2026

East Honolulu, Hawaii Kai & Kalaniana'ole Hwy

Recent Events: Severe stormy weather over the past few days caused significant damage to transmission lines serving Windward O'ahu, Hawaii Kai, and East Honolulu. A major transmission line failure occurred, temporarily knocking out power for nearly 30,000 customers.

Secondary outages were recently reported affecting over 13,000 customers across Aina Haina, Hawaii Kai, Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, Kalama Valley, and Waimanalo.

Current Action: HECO crews are actively working to restore and stabilize power statewide. Residents in East Honolulu and along the Kalaniana'ole Highway corridor should remain prepared for intermittent disruptions as repairs continue on the main transmission lines.
