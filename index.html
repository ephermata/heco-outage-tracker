HECO Power Outage Status

Last Updated: March 16, 2026, 3:41 AM (UTC)

East Honolulu, Hawaii Kai & Kalaniana'ole Hwy

Current Status: Significant outages persist in the area due to severe weather and storm damage.

  • Affected Areas: Over 13,000 customers have recently been impacted across 'Āina Haina, Hawaii Kai, Waimānalo, Haha'ione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley.
  • Restoration Efforts: HECO crews and helicopters are inspecting and repairing storm-damaged high-voltage transmission lines over the Ko'olau ridgeline. Due to treacherous mountainous terrain, repairs may take anywhere from hours to days.
  • Road Safety: Traffic lights along routes such as Kalaniana'ole Highway are affected. Please treat dark intersections as four-way stops.
