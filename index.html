HECO Outage Status: East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

Status: ACTIVE OUTAGES - Extended outages likely overnight

Latest Updates

  • Current Impact: Over 13,000 customers from Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai and Waimanalo (including Hahaione, Hawaii Loa Ridge, Portlock, and Kalama Valley) lost power again around 9:11 p.m.
  • Cause: Severe weather from a Kona Low storm has damaged multiple transmission lines running along the Koolau range that service East Honolulu.
  • HECO Advisory: Residents in Hawaii Kai and East Honolulu should prepare for extended outages lasting overnight or longer, as hazardous conditions continue to affect repair efforts.
