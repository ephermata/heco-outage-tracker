HECO Outage Update: East Honolulu & Hawaii Kai

Last Updated: March 15, 2026 - 3:41 PM (UTC)

Current Status: Ongoing Outages

Due to the ongoing Kona low storm system, hazardous weather conditions have damaged transmission lines across Oahu. As of the evening of March 14, power was lost again for over 13,000 customers from Aina Haina to Hawaii Kai and Waimanalo.

Affected Areas

  • Aina Haina
  • Hawaii Kai
  • Hawaii Loa Ridge
  • Hahaione
  • Portlock
  • Kalama Valley
  • Waimanalo

Infrastructure Alert

Warning: Hawaiian Electric reported that East Honolulu and Hawaii Kai customers were temporarily being serviced by a single remaining transmission line due to storm damage. HECO urges customers to plan for possible extended overnight outages.
